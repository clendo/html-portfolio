My Top 3 Movies I-ve watched on 2024 so far.

Cure (1999)

Japanese Thriller that will keep you guessing and theorizing all the way through, the ending is amazing!

Medianeras (2011)

Romantic Comedy from Argentina that is both creative and funny, the way the protagonists interact to each other and live separate yet nearby lives is very heartwarming.

El lugar sin limites (1971)

Revolutionary film by Arturo Ripstein, in its time it was heavily critisized for its queer elements and portraying of machismo and political themes.
